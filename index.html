About The Qatari Abaya by Teepee's

The Story

Welcome to Teepees, Qatar's premier destination for elegant abayas that blend timeless tradition with modern flair. Born from a passion for modest fashion, we craft pieces that celebrate Qatari heritage while embracing contemporary designs.

Our Craftsmanship

Every abaya at Teepees features premium fabrics like soft organza and luxurious nida, ensuring comfort for daily wear or special occasions. Intricate details—textured embroidery, crystal accents, and flowing silhouettes—elevate your style without compromising modesty. Our designs draw from Doha’s vibrant souks, tailored for the modern woman who values quality and grace.
